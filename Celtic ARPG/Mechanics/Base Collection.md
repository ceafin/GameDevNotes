## 1. Weapons & Offensive Tools (melee, ranged, magic, bombs, etc.)

**Fighter’s Sword (ALttP)** - starting sword; basic close-range slash.

**Boomerang (ALttP)** - short boomerang; stuns foes and pulls pickups.
**Magical Boomerang (ALttP)** - faster, farther boomerang upgrade.
**Bombs (ALttP)** - place/throw explosives for damage and cracked walls.
