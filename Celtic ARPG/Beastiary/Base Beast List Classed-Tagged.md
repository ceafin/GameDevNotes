## Tagged bestiary list  
  
**Tag key I’ll use:**  
- **OW** – common / uncommon overworld fauna (fields, coasts, mountains, night, etc.)  
- **DG** – dungeon standards / traps / environmental hazards  
- **MB** – miniboss / elite (tough field encounter or sub-boss)  
- **B** – full dungeon boss  
- **SB** – story-linchpin / major arc boss (also a boss, just narratively central)
